##  DAX Measures Created

To support the analysis, several DAX (Data Analysis Expressions) measures were created in Power BI to compute key performance metrics:

* **Avg Performance** – Calculates the average performance rate of interns
* **Avg Attendance** – Determines the average attendance percentage
* **Avg Productivity** – Measures the average productivity ratio
* **Avg Tasks Per Intern** – Computes the average number of tasks completed per intern
* **Total Interns** – Counts the total number of interns in the dataset
* **Total Tasks Completed** – Calculates the total tasks completed by all interns
* **Top Performer %** – Represents the percentage of interns categorized as top performers

These measures helped in deriving meaningful insights and enabled effective exploratory data analysis.
